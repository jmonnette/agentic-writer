- **Acknowledge the counterargument early**: Presenting the BCG data (74% not capturing value) actually strengthens the credibility of the article before pivoting to the 26% who are

- **End with the human implication**: Even a business-focused article should land on what this means for people, organizations, and the economy — not just what it means for EBITDA
